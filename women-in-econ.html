Women in Economics

Our Mission

Women in Economics at Indiana University is dedicated to empowering and supporting women in the field of economics. We aim to create an inclusive community that fosters academic excellence, professional development, and personal growth for women at all stages of their economic careers.

What We Offer

  • Mentorship programs pairing undergraduates with graduate students and faculty
  • Professional development workshops on topics such as research skills, public speaking, and career planning
  • Networking events with alumnae and industry professionals
  • Research presentation opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students
  • Support for conference attendance and academic pursuits
  • Social events to build community and foster friendships
