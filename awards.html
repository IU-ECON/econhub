Arlington W. Williams Scholarship

This scholarship is named in honor of Arlie Williams, a long time member of the Economics faculty, who excelled as both researcher and teacher, while also providing the department and its students with invaluable service in his role as Director of Undergraduate Studies for almost 20 years.

Award Winner: Jessica Ousterhout

Carroll Christenson Award

This prize goes to an outstanding junior or senior economics major who has demonstrated an ability to master the technical content of modern economics, intellectual interest of a wide scope, inclination and ability to devote skills to the study of social problems and issues that extend into the broader ranges of social science.

Award Winner: Amangul Hydyrova
