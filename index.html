Welcome to IU Economics Hub

For alumni, graduates, and undergraduates

Welcome to the Indiana University Economics Hub, your central resource for all things related to economics at IU. This website serves as a comprehensive platform for our diverse community of alumni, graduate students, and undergraduates.

Explore Our Sections

  • Awards: Discover the outstanding achievements of our undergraduates and the generous support from our alumni and donors that make these recognitions possible.
  • Women in Economics: Learn about our initiative to empower and support women in the field of economics, fostering a community for academic and professional growth.
  • Economics Club: Get involved with our student-led organization that promotes understanding and interest in economic issues through regular meetings and special events.

Whether you're a current student looking to engage with the department, an alumnus seeking to stay connected, or a prospective student exploring opportunities, the IU Economics Hub is your gateway to our vibrant economic community.
