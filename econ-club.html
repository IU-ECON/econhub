Economics Club

The Economics Club at Indiana University aims to provide a community for all students interested in economics and related fields. We hold regular meetings to discuss topics in economics and organize special events to promote interest in and understanding of economic issues, both for the membership and for the campus as a whole.

Get Involved

The Economics Club is open to all IU students, regardless of major. We welcome anyone interested in economics and its applications in the real world.

Meeting Schedule: Every other Wednesday at 7:00 PM in Woodburn Hall 120

Membership Benefits:

  • Networking opportunities with peers, faculty, and industry professionals
  • Access to exclusive workshops and seminars
  • Mentorship program pairing underclassmen with upperclassmen
  • Opportunity to participate in economics competitions and conferences
